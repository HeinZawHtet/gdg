Hudson Yards

​​​​​​​​​Located in the northeastern United States, Hudson Yards sits on the west side of Midtown Manhattan at the heart of New York City's major central business district. Hudson Yards is within 25 kilometers of 3 major airports and within walking distance of commuter rail lines and subways. Hudson Yards is close to major New York City landmarks such as the Empire State Building, 1.3 kilometers to the east, and CEntral Park, 2.4 kilometers to the north.
